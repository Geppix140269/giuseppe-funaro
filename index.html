Profile

A commercial operator with a builder's instinct

I am a chief executive and Managing Director with more than three decades of international commercial leadership across service-led businesses in the UK, Europe and the US.

My career has run from telecommunications and trade intelligence to diversified cross-border ventures, with a consistent thread: full P&L ownership, sustainable growth, and senior multidisciplinary teams operating across many countries at once.

Today I lead the commercialisation of a global trade-intelligence platform, engaging at board and policy level with governments, regulators and multilateral organisations. During COVID-19 I built a certified PPE supply operation from the ground up, a reminder that commercial leadership and protecting people are not separate things.

I work in English, Italian and Spanish, and I am most useful where commercial rigour meets a mission that matters.
